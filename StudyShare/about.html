About StudyShare

We believe that knowledge should be free, accessible, and collaboratively improved. StudyShare is built by students, for students.

Our Mission

Finding high-quality, relevant study materials can be extremely frustrating. You either rely on fragmented class group chats, paywalled websites that extort college students, or outdated university forums.

StudyShare was created as a modern, unified platform where notes, slides, and exam prep are democratized. Our goal is to create the largest open repository of academic knowledge.
